1. Derived Relations of Spaces

\begin{equation} \begin{align*} BaseSpace & \leftarrow Space \\ & \leftarrow SpaceWithLinearArea \\ & \leftarrow PagedSpaceBase \\ & \leftarrow PagedSpace \leftarrow OldSpace \end{align*} \end{equation} \begin{equation} \begin{align*} BaseSpace \leftarrow Space \leftarrow SemiSpace \end{align*} \end{equation} \begin{equation} \begin{align*} BaseSpace & \leftarrow Space \\ & \leftarrow SpaceWithLinearArea \\ & \leftarrow NewSpace \\ & \leftarrow SemiSpaceNewSpace \end{align*} \end{equation} \begin{equation} \begin{align*} SemiSpaceNewSpace \ni (from :: SemiSpace, to :: SemiSpace) \end{align*} \end{equation}

where \(A \leftarrow B := \text{B is derived from A}\) \(A \in B := \text{A is member of B}\) or \(A \ni B := \text{B is member of A}\)

Old generation regular object space

  1. Identifying the concept ’Promoted Pages’.

2.5. NewSpace

  1. Ability to promote pages to OldSpace.

2.6. SemiSpaceNewSpace

A NewSpace that consist of two SemiSpace (from-space and to-space).

2.7. SemiSpace

Space that can be operated by Cheney’s algorithm. For example, swap(from, to), which used to flip to-space and from-space.

3. Organization of Space

Physically, a Space is a list of Pages. Those Pages is maintained by a list of MemoryChunk. Pages is allocated by MemoryAllocator, more info at Allocator in V8.

3.1. How SemiSpaceNewSpace objects should be promoted

By AgeMark. AgeMark is an address that be setup after an gc on SemiSpaceNewSpace. All HeapObjects with address less than AgeMark is considered as survive from prev gc and should be promoted to OldSpace if it still survived in next gc.

Finally, objects that should be promoted to OldSpace can be expressed as

\(PrmotedObjects := \{x \in HeapObject; address(x) < AgeMark\}\)

3.2. Expansion of Space

The behavior about expansion of spaces is specified by Allocation Policy. In short, via a space try to ensure it’s own free space can satisfy an allo- cation with a given size by try to request to AllocatorPolicy with those size information. These requests not always succeed.

After that Ensuring of allocation, fast allocation path should succeed.
